Liz Murray

Liz Murray is an American motivational speaker and a harvard graduate despite despite having been homeless during her high school years. She was born to poor, drug addicted parents in the suburban region of NewYork. Both her parent got HIV which they contacted from using used needles. When Liz's mother died when Liz was just 15 she became homeless and despite the lack of a stable home and livelihood, Liz graduated High School in just over two years, She then went on to go to Harvard under a scholarship, and while she had to leave Harvard to care for her father, she went back and graduated, earning her degree. She was awarded several public servie awards and is a great motivational speaker.

Since then Liz has given a lot of motivational speeches, she is the founder of Manifest Living a company that inspires people give back and help other people fight and beat adverse situations. Her Memoir 'Breaking Night' became a New York Time's best seller. She also has a film made from her story called 'Homeless to Harvard: The Liz Murray Story'

So why did I chose Liz as my Tech Hero?

Her story inspires me to persevere and to believe in myself no matter how hard life may get. When life pushed Liz down she pushed right back with even greater force and that is the kind of strength of body and spirit that I wish to embody in my life
